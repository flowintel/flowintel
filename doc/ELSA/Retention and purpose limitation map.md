## Purpose limitation principles
- Data collected must remain tied to documented case or intelligence purpose.
- Export or reuse for unrelated objectives is prohibited.
- Purge schedules must align with:
  - closure of case
  - expiry of judicial time limits
  - completion of appeals

## Retention controls
- Annual review of data categories retained.
- Mechanism for secure deletion upon final closure.
- Documentation of any retention deviation, citing legal grounds.
